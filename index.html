    IIIT Sonepat


    FOUNDATION

    Indian Institute of Information Technology, Sonepat (IIIT, Sonepat) , Haryana. is the PPP Funded IIIT proposed by the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India.The academic session of IIIT Sonepat started from its mentor Institute National Institute of Technology, Kurukshetra from year 2014.

    STUDENT LIFE

    As of 2019, the classes are being held in Indian Institute of Technology Delhi, Sonipat Campus.Here are currently 2 branches: CSE & IT which accommodate 60 students each.Though campus is not too big but it is newly constructed, all facilities are there.Students have started several clubs and all are actively participating in technical and cultural activities

    Computer Science

    At present only B.tech Cousers are offered in this department, but the Institute is planning to start the same in M.tech and Ph.D too

    Information Technology

    At present only B.tech Cousers are offered in this department, but the Institute is planning to start the same in M.tech and Ph.D too
